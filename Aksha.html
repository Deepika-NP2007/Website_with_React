The Process

How Aksha_AI Works

A transparent look at the technology and steps behind every scan — from your face to your doctor in minutes.

1

Create Your Patient Profile

Before scanning, we ask you to fill a brief health form — name, age, gender, address, symptoms, and any relevant medical history. This context dramatically improves diagnostic accuracy and enables location-based doctor matching.

2 minutesEnd-to-end encryptedDPDP compliant
2

AI Face Scan

Position your face in the guided frame under good lighting. Aksha_AI's computer vision model — trained on 2.4 million dermatological images — analyses your skin texture, pigmentation, lesion patterns, and surface irregularities. The entire analysis takes under 8 seconds.

8-second scanNo image storageOn-device processing
3

Detailed Diagnosis Report

You receive a comprehensive report listing detected conditions with confidence percentages, affected areas, severity level, and recommended next steps. The report cross-references your symptoms and history for a holistic picture.

Confidence scoresSeverity gradingPDF export
4

Doctor Matching & Booking

Based on your location and diagnosis, Aksha_AI recommends verified dermatologists within your area, ranked by relevance, rating, and availability. You can view their profile, check slots, and book an appointment — all without leaving the app.

Location-awareInstant booking1,200+ doctors
Under The Hood

The Technology

🧠

Deep Learning Vision Model

Our custom CNN trained on 2.4M dermatology images with 12 condition classes. Validated against AIIMS and Manipal Hospital datasets with 98.4% accuracy.

🗺️

Geo-Intelligent Matching

Real-time doctor matching using your GPS coordinates, filtered by specialty, availability, rating, and diagnosis type — not just raw proximity.

🔐

Privacy-First Architecture

Scan images are processed in-memory and never written to disk. All data is AES-256 encrypted. We are fully compliant with India's DPDP Act 2023.
